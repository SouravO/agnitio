About The Event

The Department of Computer science and engineering in collaboration with Student association MATRICS of MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram is organizing the signature event AGNITIO'22 that provides a platform for students to tap their potential and unravel their quiescent talents. Students from various backgrounds and cultures will get an opportunity to interact and appreciate one and other. It will bestow students with an unforgettable learning experience as AGNITIO'22 offers a whole gamut of events.

F.A.Q

  • Will the college arrange accommodation for the participants?

    Yes, we assure a safer Accommodation for the participants along with food. Boys and Girls Have Seperate Accommodation facility in MES Hostel, 100/Day will be charged

  • Shall transport facility be provided by the college?

    No, There will not be transportation facilities/ arrangements from the side of college.

  • Is there a clock room arranged for the participants on the first day of the event?

    Yes, Clock room will be provided for the participants for your baggage.

  • Is it possible for one participant to attend more than one workshop?

    No, one participant can only attend registered workshop due to the scheduling of time.

  • As part of gaming sessions if any damage happens to the clothings of participants whether the college will be providing the clothings?

    No, There will not be any such considerations. The participants carry extra clothings for safer side

  • What shall be the remedial measures/first aid provided incase of medical emergencies/injuries?

    Yes,The participants can immediately contact the coordinators.

  • What are the dates of Agnitio'22?

    May 20, 21 and 22. Sessions will run from about 9.00 am until 9.30 pm.

  • How can i participate in Agnitio'22?

    Registration links shall be aired in the portal, and closeson 1 day prior to the event dates (20, 21, 22 MAY 2022) of AGNITIA'22
